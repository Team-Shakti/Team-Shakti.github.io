Upcoming!Team Shakti to conduct a Women-Only CTF "SHAKTICON"

Are you a security enthusiast? Have you ever been a part of a CTF ? If No, then here is a chance for you to be a part of
SHAKTICON. Well if Yes, then this is the best opportunity for you to test your knowledge. SHAKTICON is a Women Only Jeopardy-style
CTF with a set of interesting challenges relating to Reverse Engineering , Forensics , Binary Exploitation , Web Exploitation and
Cryptography that can be solved individually or as a team by the participants. This event is organised by the members of Team Shakti
and it aims to spread awareness of information security among the Women.
